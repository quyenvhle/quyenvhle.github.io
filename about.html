About Me


Vietnam born and raised. Philly sharpened and matured.

During the days, I’m a Management Information Systems senior at Temple University Fox School of Business. During the other days (possibly nights as well), I am a developer with perseverance, a designer with sympathy, a baker with her own recipes, and a dancer with passion.

I’m also one of those gals who cover bedroom walls with post-it notes, scribble on planners and set weekly reminders every Sunday. Yes, I love to stay busy and get things organized. Sounds stern, doesn’t it? Fortunately, my positive mind has also learned how to embrace the unpredictability. So, whether big or small, familiar or (acceptably) insane the challenge is, I’m always ready.

If you think I would be a good fit for your team, please don't hesitate to reach out.
