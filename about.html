Hey, I'm Quyen! Occasionally known as Alex.

Vietnam born and raised. Philly sharpened and trained.

I believe there always exist better ways of doing things, and strive to attain the balance between creativity and practicality in every decision.

I'm constantly looking for opportunities to grow myself professionally, academically, and personally. So, if you'd like to make something awesome together, shoot me a message!
